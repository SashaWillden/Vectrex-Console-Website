About the Vectrex console



The people who had the Vectrex console, may or may not know about the details for the Vectrex console. It was a long time ago since the release in 1982.


About the Console:

The Vectrex Console is one of the second generation consoles that was released in 1982 with a bootup screen in the feature system, but the console was short-lived. Until this day, the generation who played it has never forgotten the console of how unique it is and the console came with an integrated vector monitor, which used plastic screen overlays to emulate colours, a built-in controller and a popular built-in game called MineStorm.

There was at least 28 official games for the Vectrex console and to this date there is 32 games for the console. When using the console, if there was no game cartridge inserted, the game would boot straight into MineStorm. Other games that were popular on the releases for the system and these were Armor Attack, Scramble, Spike, Star Trek: The Motion Picture and Pole Position. The Marketing of the Vectrex Console:

Western Technologies and Smith Engineering developed the Vectrex console. The console was released in North America in November 1982 at a retail price of $199 ($460 with inflation). The Vectrex console was on put on the global market in NA in November 1982, EU in May 1983 and JP May 1983. It was distributed initially by General Consumer Electronics (GCE), but Milton Bradley brought the company market product from General Consumer Electronics (GCE) in 1983. Before the video game crash in 1983, Milton Bradley dropped the retail price to $150 and then to $100 in trying to gain a profit.

The video game console industry recovered a few years later in 1985, mostly due to the widespread success of the Nintendo Entertainment System (NES).

Technical Specifications in the Vectrex console:

The technical specifications of the console:

  • CPU: Motorola 68A09 @ 1.5 MHz
  • RAM: 1 KB (two 4-bit 2114 chips)
  • ROM: 8 KB (one 8-bit 2363 chip)
  • Sound: General Instrument AY-3-8912
  • Screen: Samsung model 240RB40
